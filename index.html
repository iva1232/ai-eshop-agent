🗺️ Your $0 Dropshipping Roadmap
A step-by-step guide to launching your first Shopify dropshipping store with organic traffic only. Follow these in order.
  • 1
    Use Product Research (this app) to find 3–5 product ideas in a niche you're interested in. Look for products that are visual, solve a real problem, and have a "wow" factor.
  • 2
    Source from AliExpress or CJDropshipping. Search the product, filter by orders (10,000+) and rating (4.5+). Aim to sell at 3–5x the supplier cost.
  • 3
    Create your Shopify store. Use Shopify's free trial at shopify.com. Pick a clean theme (Dawn is free and great). Name your store something simple and brandable.
  • 4
    Install DSers (free app) from the Shopify App Store. It connects your AliExpress products to your store and automates order fulfillment.
  • 5
    Use Listing Writer (this app) to generate your product descriptions, copy them into Shopify. Upload 5–8 product images from AliExpress (you're allowed to use their photos).
  • 6
    Set up TikTok or Instagram. Create an account with your store name. Post 1–3 short videos per day showing the product. Use trending sounds. You don't need to show your face.
  • 7
    Content formula that converts: Hook (0–2s) → Problem → Product as solution → Call to action ("Link in bio"). Keep videos 15–30 seconds.
  • 8
    First sale typically comes in 1–3 weeks of consistent posting. Once you find a video that performs (50K+ views), run more content like that. Don't change products too fast.
  • 9
    Use Customer Support (this app) to handle all incoming messages. Respond within 24 hours. Happy customers = reviews = more sales.
  • 10
    Scale what works. When one product hits $500/month, reinvest $100 into TikTok Spark Ads to boost your best organic video. This is how $0 stores start paying for themselves.
