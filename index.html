🗺️ Your $0 Dropshipping Roadmap
A step-by-step guide to launching your first Shopify store with organic traffic only.
  • 1
    Use Product Research to find 3–5 product ideas. Look for products that are visual, solve a real problem, and have a "wow" factor.
  • 2
    Source from AliExpress or CJDropshipping. Filter by orders (10,000+) and rating (4.5+). Aim to sell at 3–5x the supplier cost.
  • 3
    Create your Shopify store. Use Shopify's free trial. Pick the Dawn theme (free). Name your store something simple and brandable.
  • 4
    Install DSers (free) from the Shopify App Store. It connects AliExpress products and automates order fulfillment.
  • 5
    Use Listing Writer to generate product descriptions. Upload 5–8 product images from AliExpress.
  • 6
    Set up TikTok or Instagram. Post 1–3 short videos per day showing the product. Use trending sounds. No face needed.
  • 7
    Content formula: Hook (0–2s) → Problem → Product as solution → "Link in bio". Keep videos 15–30 seconds.
  • 8
    First sale comes in 1–3 weeks of consistent posting. Once a video hits 50K+ views, make more like it.
  • 9
    Use Customer Support to handle all messages. Respond within 24 hours.
  • 10
    Scale what works. When a product hits $500/month, put $100 into TikTok Spark Ads on your best video.
